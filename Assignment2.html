To observe the relationship between Tondu and sex, age, income, nationality, etc, we basically plotted each of these predictors on the x axis and tondu on the y axis. Our graph shows the relationship between the variables. For instance, our plot suggests that female responded more as “status decide unif in future” on Tondu whereas men were more likely to have “unification now” on Tondu. Whether ones identifies oneself as Taiwanese or not and their status on DPP membership both seem to have an effect on their unification and independence. On the contrary, difference in ones view on economy did not seem to result in any difference on Tondu.
